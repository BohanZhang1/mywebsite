Presented the tool demonstration paper for DroneReqValidator (DRV) at ASE 2023 — one of the premier venues for automated software engineering research. The demo showcased DRV's three-step wizard for configuring simulation environments, specifying UAV sensor setups, and defining safety test properties, with live demonstrations of automated fuzzy testing, runtime monitoring, and acceptance test report generation.

**Authors:** Bohan Zhang, Yashaswini Shivalingaiah, Ankit Agrawal

**Conference:** ASE 2023 — Echternach, Luxembourg, November 11–15, 2023. Pages 2082–2085.
