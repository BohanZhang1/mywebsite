Presented DroneWiS (Drone Wind Simulation), a novel CFD-based wind simulation module integrated into the DRV platform (DRV 2.0). The presentation demonstrated how DroneWiS automatically computes high-fidelity wind vector fields using OpenFOAM and a terrain scanning algorithm within Unreal Engine, allowing sUAS developers to test their systems under physics-accurate wind conditions around complex terrain and urban structures — capabilities unavailable in existing tools such as Gazebo and AirSim.

**Authors:** Bohan Zhang, Ankit Agrawal
