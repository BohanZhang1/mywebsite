Presented DroneWiS (Drone Wind Simulation) at ASE 2024 in Sacramento. The talk introduced DRV 2.0's CFD-based wind simulation module: using OpenFOAM and a terrain scanning algorithm in Unreal Engine, DroneWiS automatically computes realistic wind vector fields shaped by buildings and terrain, enabling physics-accurate sUAS wind stress-testing that tools like Gazebo and AirSim cannot provide.

**Authors:** Bohan Zhang, Ankit Agrawal

**Conference:** ASE '24, Sacramento, CA, USA, October 27 – November 1, 2024. Pages 2358–2361.
