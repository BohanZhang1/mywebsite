Presented React-G (Reflective Environment Augmented Coordinates Tracking of GNSS) at the IEEE/ION PLANS 2025 symposium, a flagship venue for positioning, navigation, and timing research. The presentation introduced a receiver-centric ray casting methodology using Unreal Engine, Google Maps 3D Tiles, and Cesium for Unreal to simulate GNSS multipath errors in urban environments. Comparative validation against NASA's NavQ real-world GNSS measurement system demonstrated React-G's ability to approximate LOS and NLOS signal path behaviors.

This work was conducted in collaboration with **Julian Gutierrez at NASA Langley Research Center** (Safety-Critical Avionics Systems Branch).

**Authors:** Bohan Zhang, Julian Gutierrez (NASA Langley Research Center), Ankit Agrawal
