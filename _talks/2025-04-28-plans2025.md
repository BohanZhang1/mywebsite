Presented React-G at IEEE/ION PLANS 2025, a flagship venue for positioning, navigation, and timing research, in collaboration with Julian Gutierrez at NASA Langley Research Center (Safety-Critical Avionics Systems Branch). The talk introduced a receiver-centric ray casting methodology using Unreal Engine, Google Maps 3D Tiles, and Cesium to simulate GNSS multipath errors in urban environments. Validation against real-world GNSS measurement data demonstrated React-G's ability to approximate both LOS and NLOS signal path behaviors.

**Authors:** Bohan Zhang, Julian Gutierrez (NASA Langley Research Center), Ankit Agrawal

**Conference:** IEEE/ION PLANS 2025, Salt Lake City, UT, USA, April 28 – May 1, 2025.
