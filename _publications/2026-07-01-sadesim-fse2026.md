Urban Air Mobility requires coordinating dozens to hundreds of sUAS simultaneously over city-scale environments — a testing challenge far beyond what single-drone simulation platforms support. **SADE-SIM** extends the DRV ecosystem to support scalable, city-scale multi-sUAS mission validation.

**Key contributions:**
- Scalable simulation architecture supporting concurrent multi-sUAS mission execution
- City-scale digital twin integration for realistic urban airspace environments
- Automated mission orchestration and conflict detection for large sUAS fleets
- Evaluation framework for assessing coordination protocols under realistic operational constraints

**Authors:** Laxminarayana Vadnala, Lucas Parzianello, Bohan Zhang, Michael Murphy, Ankit Agrawal, Douglas Thain

**Venue:** 2nd International Workshop on Software Engineering for Engineering Simulations (SE4ES '26), co-located with the 34th ACM Joint European Software Engineering Conference and Symposium on the Foundations of Software Engineering (FSE '26), July 2026.
